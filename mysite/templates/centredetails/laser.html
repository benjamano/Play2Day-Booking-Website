Laser Tag
So.. what is it??
Laser Tag is a high energy, high adrenaline, action-filled game that's main aim is to shoot the players on the opposing team, and to rack up as many points as you can!
What's the Laser Tag Arena like??
Jam packed with action and adrenaline, our laser tag arena is sure to excite and occupy the little ones, but also awake the hidden inner child in every adult who joins in!
Our laser tag arena is filled with the best UV lighting and only the best UV flourescent murals, as well as some extra laser lighting effects - Installed by one of our amazingly talented employees - that really brings the atmosphere to life!
As well as the exciting lighting, we also have pumping music, equipped with subwoofers that make the ground shake! (No, really, you'd have to experience it to believe it!)
