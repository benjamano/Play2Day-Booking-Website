Welcome to Play2Day!
Who are we?
Play2Day is a childrens play center who aim to deliver joy and happiness to children and their parent/guardians alike!
What facilities do we offer?
We have many different facilities available to accommodate all ages, including the adults:
  • A GIANT Inflatable park, one of the biggest in suffolk
  • Two large Play Structers, one included a high-tech football-pitch with digital targets to aim for!
  • A state of the art Laser-Tag arena, this is the perfect event space for a staff night out, or a quick way to tire out the little ones!
  • An always-open snack bar that offers cooked hot child and adult meals, as well as the best coffee and hot chocolate!
  • Dedicated party spaces with their very own party hosts, giving the party groups aa private room to eat their food before going off to play!
  • A specialised "TinyTots" area, designed for children under the age of 4, this is the best place for parents and their children to make life-lasting memories!
